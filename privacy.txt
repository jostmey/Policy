# NotifyLink Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 14, 2026**

NotifyLink ("we," "us," or "our") provides a communication and notification service.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and our practices regarding sharing and protection.

### 1. Information We Collect
We collect:
- Contact information (names, phone numbers, email addresses)
- Interaction data (calls, messages, transcripts, and logs)
- Account and usage data

### 2. How We Use Your Information
We use this information only to operate the service:
- Send communications and notifications
- Process and log interactions
- Maintain your account

### 3. Sharing of Information
**We do not sell, rent, or resell any personal data.**

We share data only with trusted service providers who process it on our behalf:

- **Twilio**: Handles phone calls, SMS, and messaging. See Twilio’s privacy policy: https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy
- **Google**: Provides cloud services for processing communications. See Google’s privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

These partners are contractually obligated to protect the data and may not use it for their own purposes.

**Mobile phone numbers and text messaging opt-in data** are used **only** to deliver the messages you have consented to receive. This information is never shared with any third parties or affiliates for marketing, promotional, or any other unrelated purposes.

We do not share data with any other third parties except as required by law.

### 4. Data Security
We use industry-standard security to protect your information.

### 5. Your Rights
- Reply **STOP** to any text to opt out of messages.
- Contact us at support@notifylink.com to request access, correction, or deletion of your data.

### 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy. The new version will be posted here.
